FOODATORY

A tutorial mode for cooking.

What is Foodatory?

Cooking is a crucial skill in life and is often isn't taught as a skill to children, leaving them unprepared and lacking in this skill especially later in life when children becomes independent. But what if there was a way to counter this - and teach children about food safety in a fun and educational way?

Foodatory is a collection of highly interactive mini-games designed to introduce basic cooking skills and the concept of food safety in a fun, safe and educational way. These games involve some of the core physical motions used in cooking such as stirring, spreading and cutting.It is a project designed by Team Hell's Kitchen in 2019 for the course Design Computing and Interaction Design Studio (DECO3850).
